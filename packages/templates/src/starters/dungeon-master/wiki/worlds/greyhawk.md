# Greyhawk -- Campaign Setting Reference

> The Flanaess continent of Oerth. A classic setting of political intrigue, ancient evils, and shifting alliances. Default setting for the current campaign: "The Shattered Throne of Greyhawk."

---

## Overview

The World of Greyhawk is a medieval fantasy setting centered on the Flanaess, the vast eastern region of the continent of Oerth. Once home to the great magical empires of the Suel and the Baklunish, the Flanaess is now a patchwork of kingdoms, free cities, and wild frontiers struggling to rebuild after the cataclysmic Greyhawk Wars and the brief but devastating reign of Iuz the Old.

The common calendar counts years in Common Year (CY). The current year is 591 CY.

---

## Major Regions

### The Free City of Greyhawk

The Gem of the Flanaess. A free city-state ruled by the Directing Oligarchy, a council of powerful figures from the city's major guilds, temples, and noble houses. The city is neutral ground, a hub of commerce, scholarship, and intrigue. Its famous University and sprawling Gnarley Forest border draw scholars and adventurers alike.

- **Government**: Oligarchy (the Lord Mayor and the Directing Oligarchy)
- **Population**: ~58,000 (human majority, significant halfling, elf, dwarf minorities)
- **Key Districts**: The High Quarter (nobility), Clerkburg (scholars), the Thieves' Quarter (underworld), the River Quarter (docks, trade), the Foreign Quarter (embassies, travelers)
- **Notable Figures**: Nerof Gayal (Lord Mayor), the unnamed heads of the Thieves' Guild, the Big Four merchant houses

### The Kingdom of Furyondy

A proud human kingdom south of the Whyestil Lake, Furyondy stands as a bulwark against the forces of Iuz. Ruled by King Belvor IV, the kingdom is a feudal monarchy with strong chivalric traditions. Its knights are among the finest in the Flanaess.

- **Government**: Feudal monarchy
- **Capital**: Chendl
- **Threat**: Iuz's empire to the north
- **Culture**: Chivalric, religious (worship of Heironeous, St. Cuthbert)

### The Empire of Iuz

The Old One's domain. Iuz, a demigod of immense cruelty, rules a vast empire stretching from the Howling Hills to the shores of the Nyr Dyv. His forces include orcs, hobgoblins, undead, and corrupted humans. The empire is a land of slavery, sacrifice, and dark magic.

- **Government**: Theocratic dictatorship (Iuz is both temporal and divine ruler)
- **Capital**: Dorakaa (the City of Skulls)
- **Threat**: Constant expansionist pressure on Furyondy, the Shield Lands, and the Bandit Kingdoms
- **Military**: Massive orc and humanoid armies, undead legions, powerful spellcasters

### The Shield Lands

Once a confederation of knightly orders dedicated to defending the Flanaess from the forces of evil, the Shield Lands were overrun by Iuz during the Greyhawk Wars. Now occupied territory, resistance fighters operate from hidden strongholds while Iuz's forces garrison the keeps and castles.

- **Government**: Occupied (formerly a palatinate of knightly orders)
- **Capital**: Critwall (occupied)
- **Resistance**: The Knights of the Holy Shielding operate in secret
- **Key Conflict**: Liberation vs. occupation

### Nyrond

A powerful kingdom east of the Nyr Dyv, Nyrond is recovering from the Greyhawk Wars under King Lynwerd. Once the dominant military power in the eastern Flanaess, Nyrond exhausted its treasury and military in the wars and is slowly rebuilding.

- **Government**: Monarchy
- **Capital**: Rel Mord
- **Culture**: Martial, aristocratic, with strong magical traditions
- **Current State**: Cautious rebuilding, diplomatic maneuvering

## The Mistmarsh

The starting region for the current campaign. The Mistmarsh is a vast, fog-shrouded wetland south of the Gnarley Forest and west of the Wild Coast. It is a place of treacherous ground, sudden mists, and ancient secrets.

- **Terrain**: Bogs, fens, slow rivers, mist-covered ponds, hummocks of solid ground
- **Climate**: Damp and cool. Persistent fog, especially at dawn and dusk
- **Hazards**: Quicksand (DC 12 Perception to spot, DC 13 Strength to escape), disease-carrying insects, territorial lizardfolk, submerged ruins
- **Key Settlements**: Narwell (northern edge, market town), Millbridge (small fishing village, largely abandoned)
- **Rumors**: People have been disappearing near the marsh's edge. Tracks lead into the bog but never return. Strange lights are seen at night deep in the fen. The Scarlet Brotherhood may be operating in the area.

## Major Factions

### The Scarlet Brotherhood

A secretive monastic order based in the Tilvanot Peninsula. They believe in Suel superiority and seek to manipulate world events from the shadows. Their agents are everywhere -- in courts, guildhalls, and adventuring parties.

- **Alignment**: Lawful Evil
- **Methods**: Espionage, assassination, political manipulation, magical research
- **Goals**: Suel supremacy, control of the Flanaess through puppet rulers
- **Identification**: Scarlet robes, shaved heads, distinctive tattoos (for inner circle members)

### The Circle of Eight

A cabal of powerful wizards led by Mordenkainen. They work to maintain the Balance -- preventing any single power from dominating the Flanaess. Their interventions are subtle and often morally ambiguous.

- **Alignment**: Varies (generally Neutral)
- **Methods**: Manipulation of events, targeted intervention, information gathering
- **Members**: Mordenkainen (leader), Bigby, Drawmij, Leomund, Nystul, Otiluke, Otto, Rary (former), Warnes (replacement)
- **Relationship to Party**: Unknown. May become allies, adversaries, or both.

### The Knights of the Holy Shielding

The remnants of the Shield Lands' knightly orders. After their homeland fell to Iuz, the surviving knights scattered. Some fight as guerrillas, others serve as mercenaries. Their goal is the liberation of the Shield Lands.

- **Alignment**: Lawful Good
- **Methods**: Guerrilla warfare, diplomacy, recruitment
- **Current State**: Scattered, rebuilding strength

## Calendar

The Greyhawk calendar has 12 months of 28 days each, plus 4 festival days (one per season) and a mid-year Richfest of 7 days.
